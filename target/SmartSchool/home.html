School Overview

Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Mendapat is located in the rural village of Semujuk, about 42 km from Melaka city. The school was established on January 11, 1959, through the efforts of local villagers and community leaders, starting as a small wooden building with two classrooms, 40 students, and two teachers. In 1969, the government replaced it with a semi-permanent structure, and over the years the school gradually expanded in size and facilities. By the late 1980s, more students were enrolled, including those transferred from another closed school, and additional buildings were constructed. Major upgrades took place in 2002–2003 with the construction of new multi-storey blocks that included specialized rooms such as a science lab, library, and workshops. The school continued to grow with the introduction of preschool, special education classes, and a computer lab, and in 2013 its Parents and Teachers Association received district, state, and national awards for excellence.
